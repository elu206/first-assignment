DUCK REVIEW

#1: Mallard

A standard duck. Nothing really new to see here. Points for pulling off such a dynamic pose. 3/10

#2: Surf Scoter

I've never been more afraid of a duck in my life. More terrifying than the 1986 Howard the Duck movie. 8/10

#3: Spectacled Eider

He is an absolutely monstrous little gremlin and I can't stop looking at him. 11/10

#4: Smew

Magnificent. Radiant. Unfortunately, he lacks character. He isn't unforgettable. 6/10

#5: Mandarin Duck

This is a fantastic duck. I really have no criticisms. 10/10
